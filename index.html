Crystals Game

Instructions

  1. 1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. 2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific ammount of points to your total score.
  3. 3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you loose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  4. 4. The value of each cyrstal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. 5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
